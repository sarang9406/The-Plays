Steel Magnolias 철목련

Steel Magnolias is a play written by Robert Harling and originally presented at the WPA Theatre in New York City on March 22, 1987. This is a story about 6 female and their community at hair salon. The play takes places in Chinquapin, Louisiana, which contains strong Southern English accents. On 1989, the high popularity of the play, Steel Magnolias had been produced as an American comdedy-dram film directed by Herbert Ross. The story contains both heartfelting story and comedic jokes throughtout the plots.
